A Brief Overview:

I was born in Shanghai, China.
Chinese culture, one of the oldest and most diverse in the world, has a rich history spanning thousands of years. It is characterized by its deep-rooted traditions, philosophy, and artistic achievements. Confucianism, Taoism, and Buddhism have profoundly influenced Chinese thought, shaping social values and norms. Festivals like the Chinese New Year and the Mid-Autumn Festival are celebrated with great enthusiasm, featuring customs such as dragon and lion dances, fireworks, and family reunions. Calligraphy, painting, and traditional Chinese opera are significant art forms that reflect the country’s cultural heritage.
